Alliance Rules

All alliance members must be bound to the rules, which are stated here, as well as being stated on our discord server. If a member is found to be breaking any of these rules, alliance moderators may remove them from the alliance chat if deemed necessary.


1. Respect

Respecting alliance members means to not insult anyone, and do not discriminate against them based on things such as gender, religion, race or otherwise.


2. Spam

Do not use our alliance chat to spam or disturb others experience in out alliance. Also do not attempt to troll alliance members. Trolling: Attempting to provoke a reaction out of another person or group of people.


Trading

Trading is allowed in our alliance - however you are only permitted to trade for items which are available within the trading windows. This means no trading for coupons, game codes or other out-of-game items. Substantially overpricing items may also result in being warned.


Sharing Personal Information

Sharing personal information of anyone is strictly prohibited and will be dealt with swiftly as possible.
